Translation of Poems from The Book of Songs: These are just two samples of more than 60 poems translated by Yuemin from the famous classic Chinese poetry collection.

  • “Swallows/燕燕” was originally published in Metamorphosis: A Journal of Literary Translation in 2018 Double Issue, which was dedicated to languages and literatures of China and the Chinese diaspora.
