Translations

Translation of a Cao Zhi Poem:

  • “Quatrain of Seven Steps” was published in Poets Respond in Rattle on Nov. 7, 2021.
  • Interviewed by Tim Green in the first nine minutes of Rattlecast, issue #117 regarding this translation.

Translations of Zhang Zhihao Poetry: These are translations of some poems by the renowned contemporary Chinese poet. Click the links to read them in the original publications.

  • Translation of “Song of the Pumpkin” 《南瓜诗》 was published in Tupelo Quarterly, issue 27 on August 14, 2022.

  • Translations of “Hoop Rolling,” “Night Habit,” “To the Tune of Spring,” “Spring View,” “Hand-Pulled Roast Rabbit,” “Thirty Thousand Sheep,” “In Response to My Love, to the New Year as Well,” and “Homestead on Earth” were published in Chinese Literature and Thought Today in Spring 2022.

  • Translation of “Poetry in the Open Air” 《户外的诗》 was published in Moon City Review 2022.

  • Translation of “Nobody’s Tree” 《无主之树》 was published in The Tiger Moth Review, issue 8, 2022.

  • Translations of “The Everlasting Pot,” “Fish That Jumped Out of the Frying Pan,” “By the Steamer,” “Fried Rice,” and “Fried Lotus Flowers” were published in Renditions, number 97, Spring 2022.

  • Translations of “The Spring Equinox,” “Spring at Hanyang Gate,” “End of the Year Poem 2019,” and “This Isn’t Poetry” were published in 91st Meridian on May 8, 2021.

  • Translation of “Together” & “Only to Continue to Write” was published in Exchanges on May 8, 2021.

  • Interviewed by The Cincinnati Review and was featured in “Writing about the Pandemic during the Pandemic” on April 8, 2021.

  • Translation of “End-of-Year Poem, 2020” was published in the miCRo feature of The Cincinnati Review on Feb. 10, 2021.


Pandemic Poetry Translation: These translations were originally published in The Northern Virginia Review under the heading Pandemic Sightings in the end of Oct. 2020.
